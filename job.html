Group job

Iced Tea is made from four people who have a wide choice of jobs like data engineer, product manager, software architect. Those are jobs that have a big impact on the IT industry as well as developing technology. According to Burning Glass, they have given much data about IT so the Ice Tea group will use it to evaluate members’ ideal jobs. The soft skills required by the company are in the top 5 by source labor insight jobs (burning glass technologies). However, most companies often require staff to be fluent in javascript, java, and python languages. In three specific IT skills ranked highest, the SQL programming language is not required in these companies. However, nearly companies recommend that employees have skills such as communication, organizational skills ,and problem-solving which ranks in the top 3. Finally, the ideal jobs the team intends to do appear in the top general skills and specific skills that most companies require.
