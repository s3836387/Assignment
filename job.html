Machine learning is a subfield of AI, in which the machine makes a prediction or product a result based on past data or experiences. Machine learning algorithms use statistics to find common patterns in a massive amount of data and use that pattern to make an accurate prediction. Furthermore, if the information can be stored digitally, it can be fed to the machine-learning algorithm.

There are a few techniques that machine learning used:

  1. Supervised learning
  2. Unsupervised learning
  3. Semi-supervised learning
  4. Reinforcement learning

Khai

My ideal job product manager, but since the job is not in high demand according to Burning Glass data, and I want to gain some experiences first. My second ideal job is Test analyst, which according to Burning Glass 2018 "Labor Insight Jobs" is among the top demand job (555). Furthermore, from the Burning Glass data one of the top generic skills in IT, besides problem-solving skills, is communication. I am a good problem solver but lack face-to-face communication skills. Besides that, one of the skills that surprise me in top IT skills is business skills (analysis, management, and relationship building). However, some of the top IT skills are in my skill set (SQL, Java, C#). Finally, after looking at the Burning Glass data, some of my opinions have changed, like you need a lot of communication skills and some business knowledge. But it does not change what my ideal was.
