Scope and Limits

Posted by Dung

As we have stated many time in the previous sections, due to limited time and resources, we will only do phase 1 which is motion detection. The second phase of our project proved to be too difficult and there is no suitable technology that can recreate feedback effect at the moment. In contrast to that, the suit is achievable if we only do the motion detection part, as some company have already made a similiar suit.

Tools and Technologies

As you can see on our website, our VR suit is the most developed technology to support gamer in order to improve their experience while playing games. Our VR suit used some kind of technology like Vive or Oculus with a kinetic mirror. Vive and Oculus provide VR technology software that will support the hardware part of the kinetic mirror. The kinetic mirror is a very new technology because it just has been created recently. It has been improved that very fit for our project and helps our project get optimized.

Testing

After finishing the prototype, we will let people test our suit. First, we will let our team member test and check whether it has any more bugs so that we can fix it early. Secondly, I am a member of the RMIT E-sport Club which gathers many gamers who have a strong passion for games so we can have a lot of people to help us evaluate our prototype. We have to make sure that the suit can be fit for everybody and they are all felt comfortable when wearing it. In addition, we will modify it to provide the most wonderful gaming device to experience to all gamers.

Risks

VR suit is a new and early technology. Therefore, risks always exist and we have to determine and develop it in order to keep safe to testers and users. In our project, some special risks can be caused to the users like disconnection to the suit, the signal of interacting too strong which causes injuries to users. Moreover, the suit can cause skin problems such as allergy.

Group processes and communications

We have a regular team meeting every Monday. We usually spend about 1 to 2 hours to discuss the project and our jobs. In addition, we show the progress to each other in order to improve and help to finish our work more efficiently. Facebook messenger is the most common way we use to do our tasks and discuss them with other group members. We also use something like Teams when we want to show each other our works. Moreover, we use Github to collab on our A3 and pitch presentaion.
