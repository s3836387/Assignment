Daily life of IT professional

Posted by Hoang Dung and Anh

Video 1: A day in the life of a software engineer.

Summary and explain:

This video tells us what Gyasi Linje, a software engineer, works in one day. We will learn what he does in his job to understand more about it. According to the video, he left his home quite early in the morning to go to work place. He checks his mail and meet with some internships. Then, he spends most of the time to coding. It takes him about 4 to 5 hours a day to doing it. Moreover, he also spends 1 to 2 hours to do some design about new project. He also divides the time to play some sports and relax in order to keep him healthy. I think coding is the most difficult for him while working because he has to do it alone so there will be a lot of work to do. However, he still divides the time properly to relax and play some sports which should be done by many other IT professionals to keep healthy.

Video 3: A day in the life of a Graphic Designer.

Summary and explain:

This video will show us how Shantanu Kumar, a Graphic Designer, does his job in a day. He starts his day by turning off the alarm for 2 time and waking up at 10 a.m. He may work at home because of his job may not require him to go out side too much. Then, he starts to design his logo project by brainstorming ideas which are related to the project’s context. Therefore, he can choose which one is good among plenty of idea he has created. He also heading out for a new work place. This can create a motivation for a better work and he choose a coffe shop to starts designing on his laptop. The hardest step in his progress is to choosing the most suitable colour as well as paint to make a beautiful logo for his customers. Paying attention in his work all the time is also very good.

Video 4: A day in the life of a Cyber Security specialist (Government Contractor).

Summary and explain:

We will know about a working day of a Cyber Security contractor. Cybersecurity has become so important in recent years due to the large amount of information exposed by hacker so we should aware of this problem. First, he joins a SOC meeting and then starts to work by checking his mail. After that, he begins to inspecting logs and testing sysadmin at offside data center. Next, he continues to check some high-risk log entries which was reported to him by SOC member. He has found a bug in the program running in Python code. He mostly spending his time to inspecting log in program to make sure that not any important information to get leaked outside and finding security hole.

Video 7: Day in the Life of a Twitter Software Engineer

Summary and explain:

This video follows Joshua Kcockrell, a Twitter Software engineer in San Francisco who has been working full time for the past 18 months. According to the video, the engineer leaves home for the gym early in the morning before starting to work. The office provides breakfast for their employees. He starts coding and gets help from his colleagues until noon then has lunch on the roof deck. Joshua starts his work later on and has a meeting with his coworkers later. His office offers snacks and some leisure activities at his workplace for him and other employees to relax. Joshua can also finalize his work at other places around the office before getting back home. After his working hours, Joshua spends his free time to have dinner and hang out with his girlfriend and friends before finishing his day. As a software engineer, Joshua has a healthy and balanced lifestyle as he can manage to finish his job productively, as well as spend time to relax to be ready for the next day.

Video 8: Day in the life of an Instagram software engineer .

Summary and explain:

He then gets to the office and has breakfast, coffee, and snacks at his table before having a shower and starting to work. Michael works for around 2 hours then has lunch. Michael gets back home and has a track workout for around 1 and a half hours. He has dinner and reads magazines before finishing his day. Michael spends his day mostly around the office, however, the engineer can work and relax at the same time which brings positive effects to his productivity.
